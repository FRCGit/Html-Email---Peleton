Stay Active With a Weekly Routine

We know that creating a varied workout schedule can be tough. Get started with this plan from our VP of Fitness, Robin Arzon. Built to help work your body and mind, this plan brings both the intensity and the recovery needed to help you get stronger.

MONDAY - CARDIO

Start your week with a bang—try an intervals ride or intervals run to get your heart pumping!

30 min HIIT Ride with Alex Toussaint

TUESDAY - STRENGTH

Building strength leads to more effective workouts. Take a full-body strength class, many with no equipment needed.

30 min Bodyweight with Jess Sims

WEDNESDAY - REST

Rest days are opportunities to find your balance. Recover with a full-body stretch.

10 min Full Body Stretch with Selena Samuela

THURSDAY - CARDIO

Work hard and play hard with one of our fun runs or theme rides that feature your favorite music genres.

30 min Hip Hop Run with Rebecca Kennedy

FRIDAY - ACTIVE REST

Round out your routine with restorative yoga or a low impact ride to stay active while you recover.

20 min Restorative Yoga with Aditi Shah

SATURDAY - STRENGTH

A strong core creates stability for your whole body. Try a core strength class, starting at just five minutes long.

10 min Core Strength with Adrian Williams

SUNDAY - REST

Recharge and prepare for the upcoming week with our relax and sleep meditations.

10 min Sleep Meditation with Ross Rayburn
